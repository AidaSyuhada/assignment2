Eligibility requirements

Eligibility to enter Wardatul Jannah Orphanage Orphanage:

  • 1) An Orphan (that is, a child who has experienced the death of a father or both mother and father) or Asnaf.
  • 2) Malaysian citizens who come from any state throughout Malaysia.
  • 3) Boys are between 9 and 12 years old.
  • 4) Still schooling.
  • 5) Healthy body.
  • 6) Does not have a disciplinary record.
  • 7) In dependent poor families.
  • 8) Download and fill in the provided Application Form and please email it to the address y.wardatuljannah@gmail.com OR fill out the form online by pressing the button 'Fill In Application Form' below.
