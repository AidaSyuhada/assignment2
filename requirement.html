MAIN REQUIREMENT

  • 1) Must like children
  • 2) Likes to do charity work
  • 3) Willing to work outside office hours
  • 4) Willing to work weekends if needed

SPECIFIC REQUIREMENT

Warden

  • 1) Men
  • 2) Muslim
  • 3) Family and experiences in managing children

Administrative clerk

  • 1) Diploma in Business Administration, Office Management or similar
  • 2) Or have related work experience
  • 3) Proficient in using Microsoft Office
  • 4) Can use UBS Accounts software
  • 5) Have the basic knowledge to operate the Foundation's blog and website

ADDITIONAL REQUIREMENT

Preferably candidates for wardens and administrative clerks meet the following criteria

  • 1) Has an interesting personality
  • 2) Have effective communication skills
  • 3) Have basic computer skills
  • 4) Can use UBS Accounts software
  • 5) Can speak English
  • 6) Have a driver's license and have your own vehicle
  • 7) Expert on the streets and locations in Kuala Lumpur, Petaling Jaya, Shah Alam and surrounding areas

HOW TO APPLY

Submit Your Application

Please send the application by including the information below to hr@shamawar.com.my

  • 1) The name of the position applied for
  • 2) Resume
  • 3) Latest pictures
  • 4) Relevant academic certificates and transcripts

Shamawar or a representative of the Foundation will contact the applicant to arrange an interview session
