INTRO

Yayasan Anak-Anak Yatim Wardatul Jannah was established on 19 June 2009 and officially registered on 20 January 2010 under the "Trustee Incorporation Act (1952)" in the Department of the Prime Minister of Malaysia (Registration No : PAPB 2/2010)

Yayasan Anak-Anak Tim Wardatul Jannah operates on a plot of land with an address at:

No. 1,

Jalan Rimba Riang 9/1,

Damansara City,

Petaling Jaya,

Selangor Darul Ehsan.

MAIN OBJECTIVE

To develop human values, especially for orphans and the poor in order to give birth to a young generation that is competent, has noble character and achieves the Islamic level of "insaniyah", "rabbaniyah" and "wasatiyyah"

SIDE OBJECTIVE

To play a role in building a quality society through assistance and learning and welfare programs that suit all levels of society

HUMAN DEVELOPMENT COMPLEX

This modern and complete complex was completed in 2018 and became a permanent and larger center for the development and education of orphans. This complex can accommodate up to 50 male orphans from all over Malaysia
